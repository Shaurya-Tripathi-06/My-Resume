My Hobbies

  • Playing Cricket

    Cricket has been a significant part of my life, where I've enjoyed both batting and bowling. As a right-handed batsman, I take pride in my technique and ability to build innings, while my skills as a right-arm leg spinner allow me to add a strategic dimension to my gameplay. Whether on the field with friends or in competitive matches, cricket has taught me teamwork, discipline, and the joy of sportsmanship.

  • Watch and Learn about Filmmaking

    I have a deep passion for watching and learning about filmmaking, which fuels my creativity and appreciation for storytelling. I enjoy exploring various genres, techniques, and the artistry behind cinematography, direction, and editing. This hobby not only allows me to analyze films critically but also inspires me to experiment with my own creative projects, blending my love for visual art with innovative narrative forms.

  • Coding

    My journey into coding began in high school when I first learned Python, igniting a passion that has only grown since then. I enjoy the challenge of problem-solving and the creativity involved in writing code. This hobby allows me to continuously learn and experiment with new programming languages and technologies, making it a fulfilling outlet for my curiosity and analytical skills.
